Peacocks

description location eating Fun additional Form

Description

These are a beautiful species widely known for their vibrant colors, feathers, and majestic appearance. There are many stories and folklore about these mystical creatures, let's take some time to learn more about them!

Species

  • Indian Peafowl
  • Congo Peafowl
  • Green Peafowl

Locations

Places where peacocks are found include:

  • Sri Lanka
  • Myanmar
  • Java
  • India
  • Pakistan
  • Bhutan
  • Nepal
  • Australia
  • New Zealand
  • Florida
  • Bahamas

Eating habits

This species are ground eaters meaning that their source of diet comes from things available from the ground. They are omnivores meaning that they eat both plants and animals. Their diet consists of plants, seeds, termites, ants, locust and even small reptiles.

Fun Facts

  • Female peacocks are called peahens and both genders together are called peafowl
  • Young peacock children are called peachick
